Introducing Bandcamp Playlists (Beta) Mixtapes made modern. Curate personal playlists using music you already own on Bandcamp. Give them a name, a story, and a cover—then share with the world. This isn’t passive streaming. It’s real support, real ownership, and real connection. Now available on the Bandcamp mobile app (iOS & Android). Web editing, playlist following, and more coming soon.
